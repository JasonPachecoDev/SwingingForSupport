Swinging for Support

Charity golf tournament for veterans

100% of proceeds go to

What

Swinging for Support is a charity golf tournament with the goal of raising money for the Wounded Warrior Project. This is an organization that supports injured veterans. Any money raised from the event will go directly to the organization to benefit veterans and their families. The event will gather people throughout the community to play golf, provide financial assistance, and show appreciation to all the people who served.

Why

I chose to support the Wounded Warrior Project through this charity golf tournament because of the thankfulness I have for the sacrifices veterans have made protecting the country. Growing up with close family members in the military, I have seen the physical and emotional toll that it takes to serve. The event is intended to honor those who risk their lives for our freedom while raising money in support of the wounded warriors that are returning home.

Tournament details

Itinerary

  • 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM Check-in and warm-up (Range balls will be provided).
  • 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM Shotgun start.
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Complimentary lunch.
  • 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Long-drive and putting contests.
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM Raffle and awards ceremony.

Important information

  • Registration deadline is February 4, 2024.
  • All fees are $300.
  • Event will take place at San Jose Country Club on April 2, 2024.
  • For any additional questions, contact us at swingingforsupport@gmail.com.
